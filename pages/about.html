Our Story

House Of Viyara26 was born out of a passion for traditional Indian craftsmanship and contemporary design. We believe that every woman deserves to feel elegant, confident, and beautiful in clothing that celebrates our rich cultural heritage while embracing modern trends.

Premium Quality

We source only the finest fabrics and materials, ensuring every piece is crafted with exceptional attention to detail.

Handcrafted with Love

Each garment is carefully handcrafted by skilled artisans who bring decades of experience to their work.

Customer First

Your satisfaction is our priority. We're committed to providing an exceptional shopping experience from start to finish.

Our Mission

To preserve and promote India's rich textile heritage while making premium ethnic fashion accessible to women across the country and around the world. We strive to empower artisans and create sustainable, timeless pieces that tell a story.

Premium luxury fashion brand offering exclusive ethnic wear, designer collections, and customized fashion products.
